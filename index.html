Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

My tech journey started with an ad-hoc undergraduate degree in multimedia art and music at Northwestern University. I worked in video and audio production with some web design work throughout my twenties and contributed to multiple video art projects with other Minnesota artists. In 2008 I went to Traditional Chinese medicine school and graduated in 2011. I have run my own successful small business as a holistic medical provider for over the past decade.

Sparked by the Coronavirus pandemic, I decided to re-engage in my art and technology roots. I am interested in using my adaptability, organization skills, and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code and support positive causes with my skills. I also use my eye for visual beauty to help create an aesthetically pleasing design with purpose. I am actively honing my skills in multiple domains, front-end design, graphic design, photography, and video, to have the freedom & versatility to do work remotely. I live with my husband Isaac, our cat Kal, and a small flock of chickens amongst a tiny forest of trees in Minnesota.
